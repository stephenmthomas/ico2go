About ICO2GO

ICO2GO is a browser-based tool for converting SVG or PNG files into icon-ready PNG exports at multiple sizes.

Files can be loaded by selecting them, dragging and dropping them into the upload area, or by pasting SVG markup directly. After loading, choose the desired output sizes, and click the generate button to create both the individual embedded images, and the final multi-size ICO file.

ICO2GO is free and open source. You can find the code on GitHub.
